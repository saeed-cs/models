1939 Polish artillery

AA: 40mm Bofors (wz.36 Bofors) - towed by C2P tractor - same Bofors gun most of the nations have

AT: 37mm Bofors wz.36 - towed by Polski Fiat 621 truck

Field gun:
75mm wz.1897 - towed by C4P tractor - this is the buildable version, it was the most common. Same gun French have
75mm wz.02/26 - towed by Polski Fiat 621 truck - this is for mission only, it was less common. HE weapon is identical to Soviet ZiS-3

Howitzer: 100mm wz. 1914/19 - towed by C4P tractor - this is the same gun Italians have
